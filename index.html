About the Symposium

SPARC International Symposium

The SPARC International Symposium on Smart Healthcare and Robotics Engineering (SPARC SHARE 2026) is an international forum focused on emerging research in smart healthcare systems and advanced robotics. It brings together researchers, academicians, and industry experts to share innovations and address key challenges in healthcare engineering.

The symposium emphasizes assistive and wearable robotics, artificial intelligence in healthcare, biomedical signal processing, and humanâ€“robot interaction. Author & Submission Guidelines

Author Guidelines

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research papers to the symposium. Submissions should follow the guidelines below:

1. Full Paper Preparation
  • Follow Springer's LNNS formatting guidelines
  • Paper length: 6–8 pages including figures, tables, and references
  • Include abstract & keywords, introduction, methodology, results & discussion, conclusion, and references
  • Use clear, high-quality figures and number all sequentially
  • References should follow LNNS style and include DOI links where possible
2. File Format
  • Submit PDF files for both abstracts and full papers
  • Ensure all fonts are embedded and figures are clear
3. Content Requirements
  • Original research only; no plagiarism
  • Preliminary results must demonstrate the novelty and potential impact of the work
  • Authors may be asked to revise papers based on reviewer comments
